About Leno Gulf.

Leno Gulf specializes in the processing and export of premium quality agriculture commodities.

Our company headquarters is in Dubai with 2 production facilities in the middle of vast and extensive commodities cultivating regions of Nigeria & Sudan. Both, Nigerian and Sudanese agriculture commodities are well accepted for their excellent characteristics across the world.

Due to our 25 years of experience in this field, we are capable of consistent supply, constant improvement and upgrading. This ensures us a long term relationship with our customers. We have expertise in natural commodities procurement and processing.

Also the manufacturing of processed sesame seeds such as, hulled sesame, roasted sesame, roasted hulled sesame, sesame paste (tahini), and sesame oil.

Mission Statement.

Our Mission at Leno Gulf is to supply our customers with the best Nigerian and Sudanese agriculture commodities. We are looking forward to staying at the forefront in this field and ensuring that our products meets the highest standards of quality. We seek to consistently provide our customers with impeccable service, efficiency, knowledge, professionalism and integrity in our work.


To provide a friendly, cooperative and rewarding environment Which encourages a long term relationship with our Staff and Customers.

Vision.

Leno Gulf’s vision is to be the world number one supplier of sesame seeds. Being number one does not mean being the biggest, but it means providing outstanding quality, purity, service, and value. To satisfy every customer around the world.

Quality Assurance.

One of Leno Gulf’s essential elements of business growth and sustainability is the commitment in quality. We must always follow procedures and policies to ensure that we do it right the first time, every time.
As a team, we have to improve our products and processes for the future. Leno Gulf Ltd. recognizes the value of strict quality, and has adopted the ISO 9001 and HACCP standards for its management system.
